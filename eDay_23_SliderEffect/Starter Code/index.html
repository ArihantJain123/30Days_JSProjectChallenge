Protected !!

Face coverings limit the volume and travel distance of expiratory droplets dispersed when talking, breathing, and coughing. A face covering without vents or holes will also filter out particles containing the virus from inhaled and exhaled air, reducing the chances of infection.

High Risk

Not wearing masks can make you prone to Covid 19 viral strain directly impacts the lungs, reducing its capacity and limiting the intake of oxygen and leading to ARDS and pneumonia. It is especially deadly in individuals who have underlying illnesses, and respiratory complications or both.
